Features

Built for the daily reality
of analytical chemistry.

Eight integrated modules covering the workflow from a single calculation to full instrument verification — without leaving the app, without internet, without a subscription.

Pipette Verification

Gravimetric routine verification of pipettes, burettes, and dispensers acc. to EN ISO 8655. Calculates Z-factor, systematic error, CV. Exports a verification PDF for internal records.

Air cushion · Direct displacement · Multichannel

Statistical QC

Levey-Jennings control charts with mean ±1s/2s/3s limits. Outlier detection (Grubbs, Dixon Q). Linear regression with R². Stability evaluation acc. to ICH Q1E.

Shewhart · Grubbs · Dixon · Regression

GHS Chemicals

Offline database of 80+ lab chemicals with full GHS pictograms, H/P statements, CAS numbers, storage class, and PPE recommendations. Add your own.

EU CLP 1272/2008 · Fully offline

Device Logbook

Per-instrument calibration history with sequential numbering, due date alerts (green/orange/red), QR codes, photo attachments, and PDF/CSV export.

QR scanner · Auto-numbering · Email export

Formula Builder

Build custom calculation formulas with named variables. Live fraction preview. Functions: sqrt, log, ln, sin, cos, tan, abs. Save formulas for reuse.

Variables with umlauts · Reusable

PDF Reports

Professional, signature-ready PDFs for verification reports, control charts, and analysis records. Page 2 includes a blank box for balance printout — full paper trail.

A4 · Two-page · Signature fields

Password Manager

Securely store device logins, LIMS credentials, and intranet passwords on your device. Built-in generator. One-tap copy. Always free.

Local-only · No cloud · Always free

Privacy First

App lock with PIN or fingerprint. Full backup & restore to local file. No accounts, no cloud sync, no third-party SDKs, no analytics. Your data, your phone.

GDPR-friendly · No tracking

Analysis Results

Every calculation auto-saved with timestamp and full input record. Searchable, exportable as PDF or CSV. Attach photos and notes for traceability.

Auto-save · Searchable · Exportable
